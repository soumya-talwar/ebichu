[ HOMINIDAE ]
Interactive series of autonomous artworks that model sexual aggression in great apes.
HTML / CSS / Javascript / P5.JS
Built in 2020

Hominidae simulates different forms of sexual violence and aggression observed in great apes through interactive, autonomous visuals.

The work does not endorse sexual violence; it treats “natural” explanations as insufficient and frames coercion and inequality as problems worth examining.

Evolutionary and dominance-based mating strategies are used as framing to show how aggression can escalate across generations.
