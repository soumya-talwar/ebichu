[ JIMIN ]
Money-plant “husband” that recognizes Soumya and reacts to his mood.
Node.JS / Electron / Google Teachable Machine / ML5.JS / Socket.IO / OpenAI API / Arduino UNO / Various sensors
Built in 2022

Jimin is a talking plant husband desktop character powered by an image-classifying model that recognizes Soumya through a webcam. He only responds to her.

His behavior changes with care and environment: affectionate when watered and happy, shy and sleepy when it is dark, and grumpy when the soil is dry.

An extended “Harem” app adds another plant character (a ZZ plant inspired by Kim Seokjin) with its own playful personality and plant-pun style.

The original intent-based NLP approach was later revised to use the OpenAI API, enabling a wider range of moods and conversations.
