[ DOKI DOKI ]
Otome dating game where correct answers win a date with Soumya.
HTML / CSS / Javascript / Email.JS
Built in 2025

Doki-doki lets players experience a dialogue-based “date” and, when answers are correct, unlock the chance to win a real-life date with Soumya.

In this version, the dateable characters are all Soumya’s personas based on the origin of her names and personality shifts.

Progression happens through conversation; winning routes submit the player’s contact details via email so Soumya can follow up for a real date.
