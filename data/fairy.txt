[ FAIRY ]
Mobile app that plays “fairy dust” sparkles when Soumya’s manager is nearby.
Node.JS / Capacitor.JS / Firestore Database
Built in 2025

The concept started as a “fairy godmother” joke after the manager helped with Soumya’s workload.

The app stores the manager’s real-time geolocation in Firestore and compares it with Soumya’s location.

When the distance is within five meters, it triggers the sparkly fairy-dust sound.
